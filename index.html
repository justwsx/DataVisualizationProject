Welcome to the Energy Dashboard

The Pulse of Our Planet

Energy is the invisible blood of modern civilization. Every time you turn on a light, stream a video, or drive a car, you are part of a massive global system. For the last 150 years, we built our wealth by burning fossil fuels. It worked—humanity is richer and safer than ever before—but it came with a hidden cost: the climate crisis. Now, we are living through the most dangerous and exciting chapter of our history.

This dashboard explains how and why our world is changing. It goes beyond the simple numbers to reveal the causes. Use the menu on the left to explore the story: from the explosive demand in Asia to the exponential rise of green technology.
