analytics 4. The Miracle of Decoupling

In the past, economists believed in an "Iron Law": to grow your GDP, you must burn more energy. If you stopped polluting, you would stop growing. We have proven that wrong. We are seeing Decoupling. This happens when an economy shifts from heavy industry (like steel mills) to services and technology (like software), and when machines become more efficient.

The Carbon Intensity chart proves we are getting smarter. We are squeezing more value out of every drop of oil and ray of sunshine. This is crucial news because it means we don't have to choose between poverty and climate change. We can have a prosperous, wealthy society that is also clean.
