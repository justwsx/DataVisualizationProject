The Heavy Weight of History

Why can't we just quit fossil fuels tomorrow? Because the world we built is designed for them. Coal, Oil, and Gas are incredibly energy-dense, easy to transport, and store. They are the "Old Giants" that underpin our agriculture, transport, and industry. Replacing them isn't just about building solar panels; it's about rewiring the entire global machine while it's still running.

pie_chart 1. David vs. Goliath

The charts below tell a tale of two realities. In the Energy Mix Breakdown (left), you see the sheer physical volume of fossil fuels we burn. It is a mountain that dwarfs everything else.

However, look to the right at the Global Market Share. Here is where the battle is being fought. Slowly but surely, the green slice is expanding. It is an incremental war of percentages: every 1% gained by renewables represents billions of dollars in investment shifting away from the old carbon economy.
